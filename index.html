Services

The Promise

We promise to deliver a comprehensive lead generation and appointment booking system tailored to your specific needs. Our goal is to help you achieve measurable growth by bringing you consistent, high-quality leads and ensuring these leads are ready for conversion.

The Program

Introducing our proprietary system: the Lead Accelerator. We have built a three-tier system that generates consistent qualified leads booked straight into your calendar. The first stage is where we leverage social media advertising to generate consistent lead flow. After this, we push the lead flow into the stage where we qualify the leads according to your unique specifications using emails and direct phone calls. Once qualified, we push the leads to our booking consultants where we book these qualified leads directly into your calendar without you lifting a finger.

What We Offer

1. Lead Generation Campaigns

We create and manage targeted ad campaigns on platforms like Google, Facebook, and Instagram to attract clients interested in boat rental services.

2. Lead Qualification

We qualify leads based on your specifications, ensuring that you receive only the most promising prospects.

3. Appointment Booking

We book appointments directly into your calendar, making it easy for you to connect with potential clients without the hassle of scheduling.

Duration

Our Lead Accelerator program is designed to deliver results quickly while providing enough time to see significant improvements. By utilizing a monthly retainer, this short-term commitment allows you to experience the benefits without a long-term obligation.

Pricing Model

Retainer Model: A monthly fee that covers campaign management and lead generation.

Setup Fee: An initial setup fee to begin the ad campaign.

Guarantees

Money-Back Guarantee: If we promise 60 qualified leads and only deliver 59 in a particular month, you will be fully reimbursed and we leave you with the leads for free.
